HackDucky!

HackDucky is a YSWS where you make a ducky script + a 3D case design for the rubber ducky and we ship you back a physical HackDucky board with your custom case!

How it works!

You Ship

  • A ducky script (.txt file)
  • 3D Case design (.stl and .step file)
  • Minimum 5 hours tracked on Hackatime

We Ship

  • HackDucky board
  • Custom 3D printed case
  • 8GB SD card
  • SD card reader
  • USB SD card reader

⚠️ Requirements

  • Minimum 5 hours must be tracked with Hackatime

Please do NOT inflate your time - This includes using any sort of script or method to inflate your time - doing so will get you banned

Firmware & Language Options

Using our custom firmware? You need at least 1 DuckyScript that runs on the HackDucky. You can also download Python scripts from the internet to extend functionality!

Using your own firmware? Any language is fine as long as you track 5 hours on Hackatime.
